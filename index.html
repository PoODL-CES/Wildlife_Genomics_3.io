Welcome to Wildlife Genomics 3 workshop

Dive into the world of wild genomics! This hands-on workshop is a way to make you accustomed to the cutting edge genomics tools and population genetics, all under one place and tailored for wildlife research and conservation. From decoding fundamental genetic principles to analyzing Illumina-generated Next-generation sequence (NGS) data analysis. Learn how to apply genomic insights to wildlife censuses, combat trafficking, and inform population management. With a strong focus on practical data analysis, this course also equips you to adapt the workflow for RAD-seq or amplicon-seq, if you've got a reference genome, you're good to go!.

Workshop Objectives

By the end of this workshop, you will be able to:

  • Understand and process raw sequencing data (FASTQ format)
  • Perform quality control and preprocessing
  • Map reads to a reference genome
  • Identify variants (SNPs and indels)
  • Use filtered variants for basic population genetic analysis (PCA, Admixture, heterozygosity)

Full version of the workshop is available at: PoODL-CES GitHub

Preliminary Workshop Schedule

Day Population Genomics Track Metagenomics Track
Day 01 Welcome and orientation. Basics of genomics – principles, applications, and opportunities. Sampling techniques and their impact on results. Planning sampling strategies. Fieldwork: Collection of environmental samples. Lab session: DNA extraction.
Day 02 — Troubleshooting DNA extraction. Library prep, quantification & QC, sequencing. Sequencing strategies, data formats (FASTQ). Basic Linux.
Day 03 — —
Day 04 Intro to population genetics, conservation genomics, and evolutionary biology. Intro to metagenomics – principles, applications, tools and pipelines.
Day 05 Designing a wildlife genomics project. Basic Linux. Accessing public data. Setting up bioinformatics environment. Raw data QC with FastQC.
Day 06 Mapping, SNP calling, SNP filtering (step 1). Filtering reads. Taxonomic classification. Tools: SILVA, RDP. Practical: Trimming and classification.
Day 07 SNP filtering (step 2). Diversity: heterozygosity, PI, allelic richness. Functional annotation & pathway analysis. Tools: Prokka, EggNOG-mapper.
Day 08 Population structure, relatedness, inbreeding. PCA, admixture analysis. Diversity metrics: alpha/beta diversity. Stats using R and STAMP. Visualizations.
Day 09 Demographic history tools: PSMC, GONE. Group work: Interpreting metagenomic results. Case studies. Feedback sessions.
Day 10 Selection/load analysis. Detecting deleterious alleles, selection signatures. Group presentations. Peer review. Ethics in metagenomics discussion.
